About the BMSCE

BMS College of Engineering is situated in Bengaluru in Karnataka state of India. Established in 1946, it is accredited from AICTE, NAAC, NBA and it is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University. BMSCE, Bengaluru offers 29 courses across 3 streams namely Engineering, Management, IT and across 6 degrees like B.Tech, B.Arch, M.Tech, MCA, MBA. BMSCE campus is spread over 15 Acres. Hostel facility is available for its students. Additional campus facilities such as Academic Zone, A/C, Auditorium, ATM, Boys Hostel, Canteen, Computer Lab, Counselling, Fest, Cultural zone are also there. b'BMS College of Engineering' accepts Karnataka PGCET - Karnataka Post Graduate Common Entrance Test for admission into Engineering courses

Courses

BMS College of Engineering (BMSCE) offers different courses in engineering at under graduate, post graduate, and at doctorate levels. M.Tech degree is available in subjects such as Power Electronics, Construction Technology, Computer Science & Engineering, and Bio-Chemical Engineering. Master degree holders can look for Ph.D degree in concerned subject.
